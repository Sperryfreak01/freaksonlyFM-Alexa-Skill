Great music on your Echo speaker — no phone, no menus, no fiddling. Just talk to Alexa and the underground's on. Los Angeles radio, playing around the clock.

How to get it
// The short version

It's the radio station, on your Echo. Tell Alexa to play Freaks Only and you're listening — punk, funk, disco, and all the good stuff, going 24/7. Free. Nothing to sign up for.

// Getting it

The easy way — just ask

  1. Walk up to your Echo and say: “Alexa, enable Freaks Only FM.”
    She'll find it and switch it on. Takes a few seconds.
  2. Then say: “Alexa, play Freaks Only.”
    That's it. You're listening. Welcome.

Rather tap than talk?

  1. Open the Alexa app on your phone.
  2. Tap the search icon and type Freaks Only FM.
  3. Tap the skill, then tap Enable. Done — now just ask Alexa to play it.
// Once it's on

Things to say

Alexa, play Freaks Only→ start the music
Alexa, what's playing?→ she tells you the song
Alexa, turn it up→ more, louder
Alexa, pause→ take a breather
Alexa, resume→ back to it
Alexa, stop→ turn it off

Got more than one Echo? Just say it to the one in the room you're in, and the music plays right there.
